2 What personal data we collect & how

We only collect a small amount of data. There is no account or login on this site, and we do not ask for payment information.

a. Information you give us (waitlist form)

  • Email address (required) — so we can add you to the waitlist, send a confirmation, and keep you updated about the product.
  • Coin ticker (optional) — a short text field where you can type a token symbol you're interested in (e.g. "SOL"). This is entirely optional and is used to understand what our audience cares about.

When you submit the form, your email and (if provided) the coin ticker are sent to and stored by Mailchimp, our email marketing platform. We use double opt-in: after you sign up, Mailchimp emails you a confirmation link, and you are only added to the list once you click it. If you don't confirm, your details are not added to our active list.

b. Information we collect automatically (cookies & analytics)

If — and only if — you accept our cookie banner, we and our providers use cookies and similar technologies to collect technical and usage information, such as your device and browser type, approximate location (derived from IP address), the pages you view, how you arrived at the site, and how you interact with it. See Section 5 (Cookies) for the full list and how to manage them. If you decline the banner, these analytics technologies are not loaded.

Like most websites, our hosting provider may keep basic server logs (e.g. IP address and request time) for security and to keep the site running reliably. These are not used to build advertising profiles.

We use the personal data described above for the following purposes:

4 Legal basis & consent under the PDPA

Under the PDPA, we generally collect, use, and disclose your personal data on the basis of your consent, and only for purposes that a reasonable person would consider appropriate in the circumstances. We obtain your consent in two clear ways:

  • Email opt-in (double opt-in). By entering your email (and optionally a coin ticker) and submitting the waitlist form, you consent to us using those details to manage the waitlist and send you product and marketing emails. You then confirm that consent by clicking the link in the confirmation email Mailchimp sends you.
  • Cookie banner. Analytics cookies are not set until you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. If you don't accept, those cookies are not loaded.

Withdrawing your consent

You can change your mind at any time. Withdrawing consent does not affect anything we did lawfully before you withdrew it.

  • Emails: click "unsubscribe" at the bottom of any email, or contact our DPO. You will be removed from our mailing list.
  • Cookies: clear the cookies stored by this site and/or change your choice when the banner reappears, and adjust your browser settings (see Section 5).
  • Everything else: email our DPO at joseph@altcoinbuzz.io and we will action your request within a reasonable time.

5 Cookies & similar technologies

Cookies are small text files (and similar technologies such as pixels and tags) stored on your device. On this site, strictly necessary functions and the cookie banner itself work without your consent. Analytics cookies are only loaded after you click "Accept" on the banner.

Categories of cookies and trackers used on this site
Category What it does Providers Set without consent?
Strictly necessary Makes the site work and remembers your cookie-banner choice so we don't keep asking. This website Yes — required for the site to function
Analytics Helps us understand traffic and how visitors use the page so we can improve it (aggregated and statistical). Google Analytics 4 No — only after you accept

How to manage or decline cookies

  • Our banner: decline non-essential cookies when the banner first appears, and analytics/advertising trackers won't load. You can clear this site's cookies to make the banner reappear and change your choice.
  • Your browser: most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings. Blocking all cookies may affect how some sites work.
  • Provider opt-outs: you can also opt out of Google Analytics using Google's browser opt-out add-on.

Advertising cookies: we do not currently use advertising, retargeting, or social-media pixels on this site. If we introduce them in future, we will update this policy and load them only after you accept them via the banner.

7 Data retention

When we no longer have a legal or business need to keep your personal data, we will delete it or anonymise it.
